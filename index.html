More Than Just a Button

By Jordan Sullivan

You just clicked a button. You clicked it almost automatically, without much thought behind it. Perhaps you thought to yourself, "Ooooh, that's a nice button", or "ooh, a button, I like buttons". But did you consider its complexity past its simplicity? Did you consider that this mere button was able to transport you across planes of technology and and transport you to an entirely different technical dimension to this page? Think about that for a moment. Perhaps you took for granted the power the button had. With an event listener in Javascript, often the "click" of a button can trigger a whole domino effect of actions in an application.

I aim to be this "click" in my new industry. I aim to showcase my own beautiful complexity, and in the process, empower those around me who have been taken for granted for too long in the tech industry. I will ensure that my click is heard and that the domino effect really happens.
